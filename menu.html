Yu-Gi-Oh YSIM

Welcome, Duelists!

Prepare to step into the ultimate showdown between two of the most iconic duelists in Yu-Gi-Oh! history.
In this simulation game, you'll have the chance to experience epic battles using only the legendary decks of Kaiba and Yugi.

Choose Your Champion:

STARTER DECK // Yugi
STARTER DECK // Kaiba

Yugi's Deck: Channel the mystical energy of the Dark Magician and craft clever strategies with Yugi's diverse arsenal of spells and traps.

Kaiba's Deck: Wield the raw power of the Blue-Eyes White Dragon, dominate the battlefield with Kaiba's high-powered monsters and relentless attacks.

Will you rise to the challenge and prove your mastery of these legendary decks? Get ready for an exhilarating duel where only the most skilled will triumph!

Good luck, and may the best duelist win!
